Morphology Features
===================
area: Pixel area of the segmented 2D object (in µm²).
perimeter: Length of the object boundary (in µm).
elongation: Ratio of major axis to minor axis (from ellipse fit).
compactness: (Perimeter²) / (4π × Area); shape complexity.
circularity: (4π × Area) / (Perimeter²); 1.0 for perfect circles.
feret_diameter: Maximum distance between any two boundary points.
radius_of_gyration: Spread of pixels around the 2D centroid.
major_axis: Length of major axis of the ellipse fit.
minor_axis: Length of minor axis of the ellipse fit.

Intensity Features
==================
mean_intensity: Average pixel intensity in the object region.
std_intensity: Standard deviation of pixel intensities.
cv_intensity: Coefficient of variation (std / mean).
total_intensity: Sum of pixel intensities in the object.

Spatial Features
================
centroid_x: X-coordinate of the object centroid.
centroid_y: Y-coordinate of the object centroid.
center_of_mass_x: X-coordinate of intensity-weighted centroid.
center_of_mass_y: Y-coordinate of intensity-weighted centroid.
mass_displacement: Euclidean distance between centroid and center of mass.

Texture Features
================
gabor_mean: Mean intensity after Gabor filter (texture feature).
gabor_std: Standard deviation after Gabor filter.
skewness: Asymmetry of intensity distribution.
kurtosis: Tailedness of intensity distribution.
entropy: Shannon entropy of intensity histogram.
